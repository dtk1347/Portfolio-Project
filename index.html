Table Definitions

Course Certifications

This table lists which guides are certified on river courses. Guides must be certified on a course before they can lead customers on that route.

Course Rafts

This table lists which rafts are allowed on a specified course. Some rafts are too small or big to float on some course.

Courses

Rivers may contain 1 or more courses of varying difficulty (i.e., some areas of the river may have rapids that are avoidable, which are more suitable for beginners). Guides must be certified on a course before they can take lead customers on that route. (Internally, we use “courses”; externally, we use “adventures” for customer facing marketing materials.)
